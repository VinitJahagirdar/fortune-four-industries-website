Advanced Mechanical Splicing

The Superiority of Ecofix Mechanical Splicing

Moving beyond the **old method of over lapping** (lap joints), our mechanical couplers offer robust solutions for all bar diameters, significantly improving construction efficiency, integrity, and cost.

Technically Superior

Spliced rebar performs like **continuous reinforcement**. It develops strength mechanically, providing essential **ductility in RCC structures** independent of the concrete. Proven cyclic performance offers reliability during seismic or other natural events.

Designer Friendly

Couplers **reduce rebar congestion** and greatly improve concrete flow and consolidation. This offers greater flexibility in design options, simplifies detailing of reinforcement, and minimizes fixing errors in congestion zones.

Contractor's Preference

Couplers offer significant **quality, cost, and time savings**. Fixing them requires no special skills, accelerates construction schedules, and is highly economical, especially for higher diameter rebar where lap wastage is high.

Our Products & Services

Rebar Couplers (Standard & Position)

We manufacture a full range of mechanical rebar couplers designed for full strength threaded butt splicing. Available in all sizes, our couplers ensure effective load transfer and continuity that surpasses conventional lap splicing.

  • **Standard Coupler:** Simple, fast assembly for fixed bars.
  • **Position Coupler:** Ideal for closure joints where neither bar can be rotated, utilizing a lock nut for final tightening.
  • Installation requires only hand tightening followed by a pipe wrench or torque wrench.

Threading Machines & Job Works

We provide on-site **TMT Bar Threading Job Works** carried out by Fortune FouR Industries trained operators, using proprietary banded threading equipment.

  • **Fully Automatic Rebar Threading Machine (16mm to 40mm)**: Available for rental or purchase.
  • Ensures precise, taper threading on bar ends with thread checking gauges.
  • Threading can be done on bent bars provided there is a straight length of over 0.5m.
